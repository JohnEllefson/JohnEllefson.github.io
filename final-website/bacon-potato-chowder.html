Bacon and Potato Chowder

  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup finely chopped/diced celery
  • 2-3 cups peeled and finely diced potatoes (3 cups to make thicker chowder)
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 4.5 oz Cooked bacon pieces
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 quart half and half
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp. pepper (preferably white peper)

Put the onion, celery, and potatoes in a medium saucepan. Add enough water to cover. Cover and cook over medium heat until potatoes are tender (about 15-20 minutes. Do not drain. In the meantime, melt butter and add flour. Blend and cook a minute or two. Add half and half and cook. Stir until smooth and thick using a whip to blend. Add the salt and pepper. Add the cooked bacon pieces and heat through. Add the undrained vegetables. Stir well. Makes 6-8 servings.
