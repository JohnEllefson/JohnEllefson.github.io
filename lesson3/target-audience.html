Target Audience:

Rain or Shine Weather's target audience is those who are proactive and wish to look ahead and make their plans according to the predicted weather.

Scenarios:

  • Can I take my kids to the park today?
  • Do I need to bring an umbrella to walk to school?
  • Can I fly my remote controlled drone today?
  • Will it be too stormy to go camping this weekend?
  • Can I mow my lawn tomorrow?
