Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to convince users who visit that Dry Oar is the best place to go for whitewater rafting, and is a great choice for a fun activity. The page is also intended to be informational talking about the avialiable rivers and the reccomended amount of experience required for each. One of the main focuses of the site is to get people excited about getting outdoors and enjoying time together in nature. The website theme colors and general tone will support that.

Audience

The target audience is for people of all ages who are seeking an adventurous outdoor experience. This includes both families looking for fun activities to do together, and even those thrill-seekers who want an adrenaline rush. This caters to both individuals and groups encouraging teamwork.

Typography

Heading Font: IM Fell Great Primer SC

I chose IM Fell Great Primer SC because it stands out and is a good contrast from the simplicity of Sans Serif which I chose for the font text. Also I like that the letters are a little rugged, which I find compliments the idea of an outdoor activity such as whitewater rafting.

Paragraph Font: Sans Serif

I chose sans serif for the paragraph font because its simple and easy to read. It is very common for people to lose interest if they have to much difficulty reading the chosen font.
