Why Rent a Scooter?

The beautiful island of Cozumel is the perfect vacation spot! At first many people don't realize how big the island actually is: it has an area of 184.5 square miles. Because walking is not an option, the best way around the island is renting a vehicle. Here at ~Scoots we pride ourselves on the ease of renting a vehicle. Not only is it inexpensive, but it's as easy as reserving a specific rental here on our site or just walking in our store! We also have two locations on the island so when you're done, rentals can be returned to either location.

Our Rentals

Here at ~Scoots we have a wide variety of vehicles for rent, from scooters to Jeeps. Our Honda Metro is a one person scooter that is commonly rented due to its very low cost and it's convenience for individuals to get around the island. We also offer two other scooters: the Honda Dio and Honda PCX150. These are two person scooters that are great for couples visiting sites across the island. If you bring friends or family our larger vehicles may be a better choice. The Honda Pioneer ATV and Jeep Wrangler (two door) hold up to four passengers, and if that isn't enough, we also have four door Jeep Wranglers with air conditioning that can hold up to five people.
